# OG-04 painterly scene assets

These five WebP files are original temporary production assets for the 4.4B vertical slice. They were generated from text-only art direction and do not include the supplied third-party reference image, logos, compositions, structures, or source pixels.

The files deliberately separate the scene into replaceable planes:

- `background.webp`: deep negative space and distant light field.
- `far.webp`: broken megastructure silhouette.
- `mid.webp`: asymmetric identification-array landmarks.
- `foreground.webp`: alpha-bearing edge debris; the center remains playable.
- `atmosphere.webp`: sparse edge fog and color bloom.

The art prompts requested original orbital-graveyard matte-painting layers, a small central combat-safe window, indigo/cyan/teal light, sparse orange-red heat, no typography, no logos, and no recognizable franchise design. The images are temporary; final art direction can replace these exact filenames without changing gameplay code. Layout, crop, opacity, parallax, quality tier, and fallback behavior live in `scene.json`.
